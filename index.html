What is a text message AI assistant?

Plain texting, real outcomes

A text message AI assistant lets you send natural language requests — like “check my calendar and reschedule that meeting” — without opening a new app.

Beyond chat replies

Most SMS assistants stop at suggestions. Super goes further by actually operating a computer to complete the task, with your confirmation where required.

How Super turns texts into actions

1. You send a message

Example: “Can you find that invoice, upload it, and remind me if it’s unpaid in a week?”

2. Super uses a computer

Super opens the relevant sites or apps, navigates like a human would, and prepares the workflow.

3. Cache reuse

The steps are saved in a computer-use cache, so the next similar text is faster and cheaper to run.

4. You stay in control

Anything sensitive or irreversible is queued for your review and confirmation.
